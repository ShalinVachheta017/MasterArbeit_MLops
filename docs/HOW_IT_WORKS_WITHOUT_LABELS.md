## ğŸ¯ FINAL ANSWER TO YOUR QUESTION

**Q: How does our pipeline work without labels?**

**A: Three ways:**

1. **Inference works without labels** because the model already learned from 385K labeled samples during training. It's like a trained expert - they can diagnose new cases without seeing the answer every time.

2. **Monitoring works without labels** by comparing:
   - Input data statistics (mean, std, distribution) with training baseline
   - Prediction confidence scores over time
   - Prediction distribution patterns

3. **We only need labels when drift is detected** (rarely!):
   - Week 1-4: No labels needed âœ…
   - Week 5: Drift detected â†’ Sample 500 windows â†’ Human labels 500 (not all 10K!)
   - Week 6-10: Back to no labels âœ…

**The key insight:** You don't need ground truth labels to know something is wrong. You just need to compare new data with old data!
