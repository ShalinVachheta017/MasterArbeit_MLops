\chapter{Engineering Problem Log}
\label{app:engineering_log}

This appendix records 21 engineering problems encountered during the
development of the pipeline.
For each problem the table lists: a short identifier and description,
the commit(s) in which the fix was applied, and the pipeline subsystem affected.
Problems P1--P8 are unit-conversion and preprocessing related;
P9--P14 are adaptation related; P15--P21 are infrastructure related.

  P1 & Accelerometer in milli-g; model trained on m/s\textsuperscript{2};
       14.5\,\% accuracy, overconfident wrong predictions
     & c17f3dd, 3a07e4f, 1c403f8
     & Automatic unit detection via median magnitude; scale guard in ingestion \\[4pt]

  P2 & Gyroscope data absent in some sessions; ingestion crashed silently
     & a2b9e11
     & Graceful fallback to zero gyroscope channels with data-quality flag \\[4pt]

  P3 & Scaler (training statistics) applied before unit conversion;
       normalised values still 100$\times$ wrong
     & 1c403f8
     & Move unit guard \textit{before} scaler application in transform step \\[4pt]

  P4 & Window boundary misalignment at end of session;
       last window shorter than 200 samples, causing shape error
     & d4fbe72
     & Drop trailing windows shorter than 200 samples; log count \\[4pt]

  P5 & NaN values in fused sensor stream after resampling;
       propagated silently through windowing
     & 8e3c9a1
     & Interpolate NaNs in fused CSV; raise error if NaN fraction $>$5\,\% \\[4pt]

  P6 & Duplicate timestamps in Garmin export;
       resampling produced repeated rows
     & 8e3c9a1
     & Deduplicate by timestamp before interpolation \\[4pt]

  P7 & Session with very short duration (under 60\,s) produced zero windows;
       pipeline crash on empty array
     & f3a11cb
     & Check array length after windowing; skip session with logged warning \\[4pt]

  P8 & Scaler fit on full dataset including test split;
       data leakage in cross-validation
     & 2c7d9ee
     & Fit scaler inside each CV fold on training split only \\[4pt]

  P9 & TENT training-mode forward passes overwrote AdaBN running statistics
     & f47a48b
     & BN snapshot mechanism: save $\mu_\text{run}, \sigma_\text{run}$ before
       TENT loop; restore after every gradient step \\[4pt]

  P10 & TENT optimiser included all model parameters; inadvertently updated
        dense layer weights
     & f47a48b
     & Filter parameter group to $\gamma, \beta$ of BN layers only \\[4pt]

  P11 & OOD gate threshold of 0.85 (normalised entropy) too aggressive;
        rejected valid production sessions with high class uncertainty
     & 3d1a4ff
     & Raise threshold to 0.95; log OOD decision with entropy value \\[4pt]

  P12 & Pseudo-label loop mixed raw source data with scaled target data;
        inflated in-loop accuracy while degrading production performance
     & 3fd3c00
     & Pre-process source data through production scaler before mixing \\[4pt]

  P13 & EWC Fisher matrix computed on wrong dataset;
        penalised wrong parameters and allowed catastrophic forgetting
     & a71c334
     & Compute Fisher matrix on the source-domain validation subset used
       for rollback evaluation \\[4pt]

  P14 & Self-consistency filter discarded 85.5\,\% of source windows;
        fine-tuning ran on only 14.5\,\% of available data
     & d9e0f80
     & Lower self-consistency confidence requirement; retain windows where
       argmax agrees across two passes regardless of probability delta \\[4pt]

  P15 & PSI thresholds (0.10 / 0.25) triggered on stable data;
        too many false-positive retrain events
     & bd8dc1e
     & Recalibrate to 0.75 / 1.50 for multi-channel aggregated PSI \\[4pt]

  P16 & Duplicate trigger fires within same batch; cooldown not enforced;
        retraining loop entered twice
     & e8c2b5f
     & Add cooldown state to trigger policy; reject trigger if within 24 hours
       of most recent activation \\[4pt]

  P17 & MLflow tracking server unavailable during CI run;
        pipeline crashed on \texttt{MlflowException}
     & 7a44d19
     & Wrap all MLflow calls in try/except; fall back to local JSON log \\[4pt]

  P18 & Docker image missing \texttt{/app/src} on PYTHONPATH;
        import errors at API startup
     & 9c4e00b
     & Set \texttt{ENV PYTHONPATH=/app:/app/src} in Dockerfile \\[4pt]

  P19 & \texttt{promote\_to\_shared} flag defaulting to True in nightly CI;
        test-adapted model promoted to shared baseline
     & b1f2e01
     & Set governance flag to False in CI environment variable; require
       explicit override for production promotion \\[4pt]

  P20 & \texttt{pytest} slow tests timed out in CI (TF model load $>$~60\,s)
     & 4c8d9a3
     & Mark TF-dependent tests with \texttt{@pytest.mark.slow};
       run in separate CI job with 360\,s timeout \\[4pt]

  P21 & Artefact directories accumulated indefinitely; disk usage exceeded
        quota in long-running development environment
     & ---
     & Added \texttt{cleanup\_repo.ps1} script (PowerShell) to archive
       artefacts older than 30 days \\[4pt]
