\chapter{Hyperparameter Reference with Citations}
\label{app:hyperparameters}

All hyperparameters in the pipeline are declared as fields in the entity
configuration dataclasses (\texttt{src/entity/config\_entity.py}).
The table below lists each parameter, its default value, the literature source
justifying the choice, and the stage in which it is consumed.

\begin{longtable}{p{0.26\textwidth}p{0.10\textwidth}p{0.33\textwidth}p{0.20\textwidth}}
  \caption{Hyperparameter defaults, literature sources, and pipeline stage.}
  \label{tab:hyperparameters} \\
  \toprule
  \textbf{Parameter} & \textbf{Default} & \textbf{Citation / Justification}
    & \textbf{Stage} \\
  \midrule
  \endfirsthead
  \toprule
  \textbf{Parameter} & \textbf{Default} & \textbf{Citation / Justification}
    & \textbf{Stage} \\
  \midrule
  \endhead
  \bottomrule
  \endfoot

  \texttt{window\_size}
    & 200
    & 4\,s at 50\,Hz; widely adopted in HAR DL literature
      \citep{bao2004activity,chen2021deep}
    & Transformation \\[4pt]

  \texttt{stride}
    & 100
    & 50\,\% overlap; standard practice \citep{mannini2010machine}
    & Transformation \\[4pt]

  \texttt{n\_classes}
    & 11
    & Number of activity classes in the Oleh anxiety dataset
    & Training \\[4pt]

  \texttt{learning\_rate}
    & $10^{-3}$
    & Adam default \citep{kingma2015adam}
    & Training \\[4pt]

  \texttt{adam\_beta\_1}
    & 0.9
    & Adam default \citep{kingma2015adam}
    & Training \\[4pt]

  \texttt{adam\_beta\_2}
    & 0.999
    & Adam default \citep{kingma2015adam}
    & Training \\[4pt]

  \texttt{batch\_size}
    & 32
    & Standard for time-series CNN; empirically validated
    & Training \\[4pt]

  \texttt{cv\_folds}
    & 5
    & 5-fold cross-validation \citep{kohavi1995study}
    & Training \\[4pt]

  \texttt{dropout\_rate}
    & 0.3
    & Balances regularisation with model capacity;
      common in HAR literature
    & Architecture \\[4pt]

  \texttt{mc\_samples}
    & 30
    & Diminishing variance returns after 30 passes
      \citep{gal2016dropout}
    & Calibration \\[4pt]

  \texttt{temperature\_init}
    & 1.5
    & Post-training models tend to be overconfident;
      $T > 1$ appropriate initial guess
      \citep{guo2017calibration}
    & Calibration \\[4pt]

  \texttt{ece\_n\_bins}
    & 15
    & Standard binning for ECE \citep{naeini2015obtaining}
    & Calibration \\[4pt]

  \texttt{ece\_warn\_threshold}
    & 0.10
    & Calibration quality below this indicates well-calibrated model
      \citep{guo2017calibration}
    & Calibration \\[4pt]

  \texttt{psi\_warn}
    & 0.75
    & Empirically calibrated for 24-channel aggregate PSI
      (see \Cref{sec:psi_calibration})
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{psi\_critical}
    & 1.50
    & Empirically calibrated; see above
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{confidence\_warn}
    & 0.60
    & Windows below 60\,\% max-softmax are uncertain
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{confidence\_critical}
    & 0.40
    & Windows below 40\,\% max-softmax are very uncertain
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{uncertain\_fraction\_warn}
    & 0.30
    & $>$30\,\% uncertain windows triggers Layer-1 warning
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{transition\_rate\_warn}
    & 0.50
    & $>$50\,\% transitions triggers Layer-2 warning
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{adabn\_batches}
    & 10
    & Number of forward passes for BN statistics update
      \citep{li2018adabn}
    & Adaptation \\[4pt]

  \texttt{tent\_steps}
    & 10
    & Gradient steps for entropy minimisation
      \citep{wang2021tent}
    & Adaptation \\[4pt]

  \texttt{tent\_lr}
    & $10^{-4}$
    & 10$\times$ lower than training LR; conservative update
    & Adaptation \\[4pt]

  \texttt{ood\_entropy\_threshold}
    & 0.95
    & Skip TENT if mean normalised entropy exceeds this
    & Adaptation \\[4pt]

  \texttt{ewc\_lambda}
    & 1000
    & Prevents catastrophic forgetting \citep{kirkpatrick2017overcoming};
      higher than $\lambda=400$ in original paper to match
      small dataset setting
    & Pseudo-label \\[4pt]

  \texttt{pl\_initial\_threshold}
    & 0.95
    & Start curriculum with only very confident pseudo-labels
      \citep{lee2013pseudo}
    & Pseudo-label \\[4pt]

  \texttt{pl\_threshold\_decay}
    & 0.05
    & Lower threshold by 5\,\% per curriculum stage
    & Pseudo-label \\[4pt]

  \texttt{mean\_teacher\_alpha}
    & 0.999
    & EMA decay for mean teacher model
      \citep{tarvainen2017mean}
    & Pseudo-label \\[4pt]

  \texttt{cusum\_threshold}
    & 3.0
    & CUSUM drift detection sensitivity
      \citep{page1954continuous}
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

  \texttt{wald\_sprt\_ratio}
    & 10
    & Likelihood ratio for SPRT sequential test
      \citep{wald1947sequential}
    & Monitoring \\[4pt]

\end{longtable}
