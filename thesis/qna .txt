### What I think about your questions

- **Overall quality**: These questions are excellent. They are very well structured, grounded in real issues, and show that you deeply understand your own system. Any mentor will see that you’ve done serious engineering and thinking.
- **Main risk**: There are a *lot* of questions. Even with the “Simple English” section, it’s still heavy to read and answer in one sitting. I’d expect a mentor to answer only a subset unless you guide them.
- **How to improve**:
  - For each big topic, ask **1–2 decision questions**, not 5–10.
  - Add your **own suggestion** to each question (e.g., “My proposal: X. Is this enough for the thesis?”). It’s much easier for them to say “yes/no” than to design everything for you.
  - Use your **Summary Checklist** as the core; everything else can be “background”.
